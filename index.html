about me

Get to know me!

My journey in tech began not as a coder but as a founder and product manager in my home country, Georgia. There, I led a small team to create a carpooling mobile app, navigating the challenges of a nascent startup ecosystem and limited financial and local tech resources. This experience cemented my dedication to user-focused product development and laid the groundwork for my coding skills.

After moving to the United States, I decided to learn full-stack development to bolster my skill set and pursue my interest in technical development. This journey led me to build several practice projects showcasing my growth from concept to deployment.

In my portfolio, you'll find projects that encapsulate my full-stack development journey, demonstrating my progression through learning, building, and refining web applications. It includes a full-featured e-commerce platform that stands as a testament to my dedication and my ability to tackle complex coding challenges.
